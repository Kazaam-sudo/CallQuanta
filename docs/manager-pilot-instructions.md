# Manager pilot instructions

Use the CallQuanta pilot link and credentials provided by the project admin.

## Steps

1. Open the assigned CallQuanta pilot URL.
2. Log in with your email and temporary password.
3. Open **QA Reviews** or the review links assigned to you. If you upload pilot recordings, supported formats include WAV, MP3, M4A, OGG/Opus, FLAC, and WebM.
4. Open the call details **Overview** and play **Call recording**. Compare the original audio with the transcript and AI review while validating the call.
5. Check the call transcript for accuracy, speaker clarity, and missing or incorrect words.
6. Check the AI review score, evidence, findings, and recommendations.
7. Add your human review: confirm what is correct, correct what is wrong, and add short notes where useful.
8. Add a coaching action when the call shows a clear behavior to improve.
9. Submit feedback on whether the AI review was useful and whether transcript or QA logic problems affected the review.
10. Export reviews only if your pilot lead asks for an export.

If the browser cannot play an OGG/Opus or other recording, select **Download audio** in the recording card and use an approved local player. Audio playback and download use the same scoped call permissions as the rest of CallQuanta.

## What feedback to report

Tell the pilot lead about:

- Login or access problems.
- Missing assigned reviews.
- Slow pages or uploads.
- Transcript mistakes that affect scoring. OGG/Opus pilot uploads are supported, but if one recording cannot be transcribed, try WAV/MP3 or report the file as an STT issue.
- AI scores that do not match the call.
- AI evidence that is missing, weak, or incorrect.
- Coaching suggestions that are not useful.
- Any sensitive-data concern.

Use only approved test recordings in this pilot environment.

## Тематики звонков для менеджеров

В версии v0.25.0 на странице звонка появился блок **Тематика звонка / Call topic**. Он показывает основную тему, уверенность классификации, дополнительные темы, цитаты-доказательства и выполнение обязательных действий для выбранной тематики. Менеджер или супервайзер может исправить тему вручную, если AI-классификация неверна; исправленная тема используется в экспортах и аналитике.

Для проверки пилота: откройте звонок с транскриптом, убедитесь, что тема определена, просмотрите список обязательных действий и отметьте, какие действия оператор пропустил. Общая QA-оценка остается отдельной от тематического compliance.
